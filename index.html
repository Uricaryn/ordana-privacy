Privacy Policy

Ordana Mobile Application

Last updated: 15 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Ordana (the "Application") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Email address and user-provided profile information (if any)
  • Account Information: Authentication data managed via Firebase Authentication
  • Usage Data: IP address, device type, operating system, app interactions
  • Crash & Diagnostics: Error logs and performance data collected via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Advertising Data: Advertising ID and ad interaction data collected via Google AdMob

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Application
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and fix technical issues
  • To display advertisements
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Ordana uses the following third-party services, which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – authentication, analytics, crash reporting, backend services
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) – advertising and ad performance measurement

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Information

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not sold or rented to third parties. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers when necessary to operate the Application or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable laws.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent where applicable.

8. Children’s Privacy

Ordana does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Advertising

Ordana displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers to show personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google’s policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately.
